Product Introduction

Polycarbonate (PC) Sheet is a high-performance transparent engineering thermoplastic that combines exceptional impact resistance with optical clarity. With an impact strength approximately 250 times greater than glass and 30 times greater than acrylic (PMMA), PC sheet is the material of choice for safety glazing, machine guards, and protective barriers.

PC sheet transmits up to 88% of visible light while blocking UV radiation (UV-stabilized grades), making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications. Its wide operating temperature range (-40°C to +120°C) and dimensional stability ensure reliable performance across diverse environmental conditions. Jiasheng Plastic offers solid PC sheet, twin-wall (hollow) PC sheet, and corrugated PC sheet for various architectural and industrial applications.
