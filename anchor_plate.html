Product Introduction

Polyethylene Anchor Plates are specialized geotechnical components used to secure geomembranes, geotextiles, and other geosynthetic materials in civil engineering, environmental containment, and mining applications. Manufactured from high-density polyethylene (HDPE) or UHMWPE, these plates distribute anchor loads over a larger area, preventing pull-through failure of geomembrane liner systems.

PE anchor plates are designed to work with standard anchor bolts, rock bolts, and soil nails, providing a corrosion-resistant, chemically inert clamping surface that protects the geomembrane from point loading and UV degradation at anchor points. Their lightweight nature simplifies installation in remote or difficult-to-access locations.
