Product Introduction

Acrylic Sheet, also known as PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate) or by brand names Plexiglass® and Perspex®, is a transparent thermoplastic renowned for its exceptional optical clarity, UV resistance, and weatherability. With a light transmission of up to 92% (higher than glass), acrylic sheet provides superior optical performance for display, signage, and architectural applications.

Acrylic sheet is available in cast and extruded forms, each offering distinct advantages. Cast acrylic provides better optical quality, superior chemical resistance, and excellent machinability, making it preferred for high-end displays and precision components. Extruded acrylic offers tighter thickness tolerances and is more economical for large-volume applications. Jiasheng Plastic offers both grades in a comprehensive range of colors, thicknesses, and surface finishes.
