Product Introduction

Flame Retardant Polypropylene Sheet is a specially formulated PP grade that incorporates halogen-free flame retardant additives to achieve UL94 V-0 or V-2 fire ratings while maintaining the excellent chemical resistance and mechanical properties of standard polypropylene. This material addresses the critical need for fire safety in chemical processing, electrical, and transportation applications where standard PP's flammability is a concern.

The halogen-free flame retardant system used by Jiasheng Plastic ensures compliance with RoHS and REACH regulations while providing reliable fire performance. The material self-extinguishes within 10 seconds of flame removal (V-0 rating) and produces minimal smoke and toxic gases during combustion, improving safety in enclosed environments.
